# Mosaic figure caption

![Commercial powder-dispensing landscape mosaic](./mosaic.png)

**Figure.** Visual landscape of the commercial powder-dispensing platforms discussed in [`../commercial-powder-dispensing-landscape.md`](../commercial-powder-dispensing-landscape.md). The figure compactly arranges 40 representative product photographs in six thematic groups (coloured banners): bench-top automated lab dispensers; liquid-handling / balance platforms with optional solid-dispensing modules; industrial loss-in-weight (LIW) and bulk powder handling; the two unrelated MTI companies (MTI Corporation lab equipment vs MTI Mischtechnik industrial mixers); specialised micro-dose feeders compared in Hou *et al.* (2024); and acoustic / piezo dispensers (predominantly liquid, included for context). Panels are labelled (a)–(an) in reading order and per-panel sources are listed below; complete metadata including image and source URLs is in [`_manifest.json`](./_manifest.json) and [`README.md`](./README.md). Images are reproduced for non-commercial editorial / research reference; all rights remain with the respective vendors.
